Qworkery

A funky, independent, co-working space in the centre of Skipton. The opportunities of the space at Qworkery are varied and explorative and we’d love to hear any ideas. Some possibilities include:
• Lunchtime ‘scratch performances’ – 10-15 minute performances to explore new work
• Gallery or installation space
• Evening/Weekend workshop/collaboration space
• Well-being offers for users of Qworkery (lunchtime, early morning, evening)
