Collaborations

We think collaboration is where the magic happens! We’d love to bring creatives and artists together for collaboration and we’re also interested in teaming artists with cross-sector specialists. If you’re an artist who is interested in exploring a certain topic (environmental issues for example) please get in touch. Equally if you think your specialist subject would benefit from a new and creative interpretation we’d love to hear from you – get in touch via our contact page.

Yarndale

Watch this Space is teaming up with Yarndale to create an installation celebrating yarn and its heritage in Skipton. There will be community workshops and the opportunity for everyone to make something that becomes part of the artwork. If you are a local textile artist, crafter, maker or interested in hosting workshops or part of the artwork please get in touch – more details on this project coming soon!

Craven Music Match

‘Craven Music Match’ aims to support up and coming musicians with workshops and collaboration with other musicians and performance opportunities. Led by local musician, Sarah Smout, Watch this Space will be helping to find suitable venues for 1-day collaborations, practices, performance and filming.
